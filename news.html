Mangrove Restoration Project Launched in Puttalam District

January 15, 2024 | Conservation Community

A new community-led mangrove restoration initiative has been launched in the Puttalam lagoon area, aiming to replant 50,000 mangrove saplings over the next two years. The project involves local fishing communities and aims to restore critical coastal protection while creating sustainable livelihoods.

December 3, 2023 Research

Record Number of Migratory Birds Spotted in Bundala

Bird watchers and researchers recorded over 15,000 migratory birds in Bundala National Park this season, the highest count in the past decade. The increase is attributed to improved wetland management and reduced human disturbance.

November 18, 2023 Research Environment

New Study: Colombo Wetlands Filter 40% of City's Wastewater

Research from the University of Colombo reveals that the Muthurajawela and Bellanwila-Attidiya wetlands naturally filter and purify approximately 40% of Colombo's wastewater, saving millions in treatment costs.

October 29, 2023 Education Community

Wetland Education Program Reaches 5,000 Students

The "Wetland Guardians" school program has now educated over 5,000 students across 50 schools about wetland conservation. The program includes field visits, workshops, and citizen science projects.

September 12, 2023 Climate Policy

Sri Lanka's Wetlands to Play Key Role in Climate Adaptation Plan

The Ministry of Environment has included wetland restoration as a key component of Sri Lanka's National Climate Change Adaptation Strategy, recognizing their role in flood mitigation and carbon sequestration.

New Endemic Fish Species Discovered in Knuckles Wetlands
